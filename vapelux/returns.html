Returns & Refunds

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We're here to help.

30-Day Money Back Guarantee

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return unopened and unused products within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Defective Items

Manufacturer defects are covered for 90 days from the date of purchase. If your device is malfunctioning, please contact our support team immediately for a replacement.

Non-Returnable Items

For health and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns on the following items once they have been opened:

  • E-liquids
  • Coils and Pods
  • Disposable Vapes
  • Batteries

How to Start a Return

To initiate a return, please email returns@vapelux.com with your order number and the reason for the return. We will provide you with a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) and shipping instructions.
