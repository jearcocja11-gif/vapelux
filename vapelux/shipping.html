Shipping & Delivery

Fast, discreet, and reliable shipping to your doorstep.

Discreet Packaging

All orders are shipped in plain, unmarked boxes or mailers. The return address will show "VL Logistics" to ensure your privacy.

Due to federal regulations, all vape shipments require an adult signature (21+) upon delivery. Please ensure someone is available to sign for your package.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can monitor your shipment through our website or the carrier's portal.
